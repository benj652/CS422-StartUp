Plan your college career with more clarity.

Blueprint.edu helps students figure out what to do and when to do it. Instead of piecing together advice from many places, students can use one planner to explore major-specific roadmaps, recommended classes, and opportunities to pursue throughout college.

Start planning View dashboard

What this MVP includes

  • Major selection for CS or Economics
  • Class year selection from freshman to senior
  • Roadmap pages with classes, programs, and resources
  • A cleaner, more guided planning experience

How it works

This first version is focused on helping students quickly move from uncertainty to a clearer next step.

1. Choose your path

Select a major and class year to begin the planning process.

2. View a roadmap

See classes, opportunities, and suggested steps organized in one place.

3. Use it as a guide

Turn scattered advice into a simpler year-by-year plan you can follow.
