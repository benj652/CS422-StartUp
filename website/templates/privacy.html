Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

About This Project

Blueprint is an experimental prototype developed as part of a Colby's software engineering course (CS422). It is not a commercial product. Any data collected is used solely for educational research and product improvement purposes.

What Data We Collect

When you use Blueprint, we collect the following information:

  • Academic profile: Your class year, major, career goal, career stage, and current priority — submitted voluntarily through the onboarding form.
  • Usage data: Pages visited and interactions (e.g. button clicks, form submissions), logged anonymously.
  • Session identifier: An anonymous UUID stored in a cookie to link your visits across sessions. No name, email, or personally identifiable information is collected.

Why We Collect It

  • To generate a personalised academic roadmap based on your answers.
  • To understand how users interact with the product and improve it.
  • To validate the product concept as part of our course research.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only within the scope of this course project. It is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising. Access is limited to the student development team and course instructors.

Data Retention

Data is retained for the duration of the course and will be deleted or anonymised at the end of the project. You may request early deletion at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any data associated with your session. To make a request, please contact the development team at your institution. Because we do not collect your name or email, you will need to provide your anonymous session identifier (found in your browser's cookies for this site).

Limitations

Blueprint is an experimental prototype. The roadmaps and recommendations it provides are not professionally verified and should not be used as the sole basis for academic or career decisions. Please consult your academic advisor for official guidance.
