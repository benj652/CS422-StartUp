Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser by a website. Blueprint uses a minimal number of cookies strictly necessary to provide the service.

Cookies We Use

Cookie Purpose Duration Type
Session cookie Stores an anonymous identifier to link your visits across sessions, enabling personalised roadmap delivery and anonymous usage analytics. 30 days First-party, functional

What We Don't Do

  • We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.
  • We do not share cookie data with any external services.
  • We do not use cookies to identify you personally.

Analytics

We log anonymous page visits and interactions (such as form submissions) tied to your session cookie. This data helps us understand how the product is used and improve it. All analytics are stored on our own servers and are not shared with third parties.

Managing Cookies

You can delete or block cookies at any time through your browser settings. If you clear your session cookie, a new anonymous session will be created on your next visit and any previously saved preferences will not be recoverable.
